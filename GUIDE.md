# User Guide - AI Career Guidance Assistant

## Getting Started

Welcome to the AI Career Guidance Assistant! This tool helps students discover career paths that match their interests and education.

### Step 1: Visit the Home Page

When you first open the app, you'll see the landing page with:
- An overview of what the tool offers
- Quick access buttons to start your journey
- Industry categories you can explore

### Step 2: Complete Your Profile

Click **"Start Your Journey"** to access the profile form. You'll need to provide:

1. **Education Level** - Select your current education:
   - High School
   - Undergraduate (Bachelor's)
   - Graduate (Master's/Ph.D.)
   - Working Professional

### Step 3: Get Your Recommendations

After submitting, the AI will analyze your profile and provide:

- **Career Matches** - Personalized career recommendations with match scores
- **Job Details** - Salary ranges, growth outlook, and required education
- **Skills Needed** - Key competencies for each career path
- **Study Tips** - Guidance specific to your chosen field
- **Next Steps** - Actionable plan to pursue your goals
- **Learning Resources** - Links to educational platforms

### Step 4: Explore and Retake

- Switch between tabs to view different recommendations
- Click "Start Over" to retake the assessment with new information
- Visit the "About" page to learn more about how the tool works

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be Honest** - Provide accurate information about your interests
2. **Select Multiple Interests** - Don't limit yourself to just one field
3. **Be Detailed** - The more specific you are about your skills, the better the recommendations
4. **Explore All Options** - Review all recommended careers, not just the top match

## Privacy

Your data is stored **only** in your browser's localStorage:
- We don't collect any personal information
- No data is sent to any servers
- Clear your browser data to remove all stored information

## Troubleshooting

**Q: My recommendations didn't load?**
A: Refresh the page and try again. Make sure to complete all required fields.

**Q: How do I update my profile?**
A: Click "Start Over" on the results page to retake the assessment.

**Q: Are these real job predictions?**
A: Our recommendations are based on common career pathways but should be used as a starting point. Always consult with career professionals.
